Lua C-API functions by category

Functions may appear in multiple categories.

The prefixes lua_ and luaL_ are omitted. Some function pairs (e.g. lua_len and luaL_len) only differ in the prefix. In this cases look if the function is marked as being in the “Auxiliary library” (aux) category.
